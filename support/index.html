App Support

The MenloHacks app is intended for attendees of MenloHacks IV, the Menlo School hackathon from March 9-10, present at the event. If you are at the event and experiencing issues, please find an organizer. You can also email hello@menlohacks.com for immediate support.

The app should show the schedule, any announcements that have been made, and allow you to check-in to the event using Apple Wallet. Moreover, there is a mentorship system to allow you to help find a mentor. For questions about features, please find an organizer at the event.
